## About Me

I am an incoming PhD student in Computer Science at [Georgia Tech](https://www.gatech.edu/), where I will be advised by [Qirun Zhang](https://helloqirun.github.io/). My research interests are in cryptography, programming languages, and formal verification, with a current focus on program analysis for finite-field programs. 
<br><br>
Previously, I worked as a software engineer at [Amuse Labs](https://amuselabs.com/). I graduated summa cum laude from [Ashoka University](https://www.ashoka.edu.in/) with a degree in Computer Science and Mathematics. During my undergraduate studies, I worked with research groups at the [Max Planck Institute for Software Systems](https://mpi-dsg.org/), [IIT Delhi](https://home.iitd.ac.in/), and [IIT Gandhinagar](https://iitgn.ac.in/faculty/ee/fac-joycee) on problems in data systems, cryptography, and hardware-aware optimization.
