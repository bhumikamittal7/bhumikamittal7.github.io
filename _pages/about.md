## About Me

I work as a software engineer at [Amuse Labs](https://amuselabs.com/). My research focuses on problems in cryptography, programming languages, and formal verification. 
<br><br>
I graduated summa cum laude from [Ashoka University](https://www.ashoka.edu.in/) with a degree in Computer Science and Mathematics. My undergraduate thesis, [Ring Trapdoor Functions: A Lattice-Based Framework for Secure Ring Signatures](/assets/pdf/undergrad_thesis_bhumika.pdf), developed a framework for constructing secure ring signatures from lattice assumptions. During this time, I also held research positions at the [Max Planck Institute for Software Systems](https://mpi-dsg.org/), [IIT Delhi](https://home.iitd.ac.in/), and [IIT Gandhinagar](https://iitgn.ac.in/faculty/ee/fac-joycee), working on problems across data systems, cryptography, and hardware-aware optimization.
