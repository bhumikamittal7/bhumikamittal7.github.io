Undergrad Architecture Mentoring Workshop (uArch)

I was fortunate enough to attend uArch 2024, a day-long workshop focused on informing students about graduate studies, particularly in computer architecture and the application process for grad school. I documented the advice shared during the various sessions, which I believe is valuable and worth learning from. My intention in drafting this is to share these insights in the hope that they will help other applicants. If you have any suggestions or spot any inaccuracies, please write to me at mittalbhumika7@gmail.com.
